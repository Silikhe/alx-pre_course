Chlorox App Redesign

To begin, it was really important to know where things stood and understand who was involved, their goals, and the issues they were dealing with. I teamed up with our Product Owner to figure out where to get the best info and who might care about a solution like this. By chatting with a bunch of people—engineers who put Dafric’s ideas into their work, customers using Dafric, and drivers who are basically the face of these changes—I got a clear picture of how people go through using this stuff and the problems they run into. I was able to map the existing user journey and identify key pain points they encountered along the way.
